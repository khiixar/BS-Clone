Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy? +
We offer free shipping on all U.S. orders over $50. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. International shipping is available and rates are calculated at checkout based on your location.
What is your return policy? +
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items with original tags attached. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item.
How do I know what size to order? +
We provide detailed size charts on each product page. Our garments are designed with an oversized, relaxed fit. If you're between sizes or prefer a more fitted look, we recommend sizing down. For specific measurements, please refer to the size guide or contact our support team.
What materials are your products made from? +
We use premium heavyweight cotton and fleece materials. Our standard tees are made from 100% cotton, while our hoodies and sweatpants feature a cotton-polyester blend for durability and comfort. All materials are carefully selected for their quality and longevity.
Do you offer wholesale pricing? +
Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please visit our Wholesale page or contact us directly at wholesale@blanksaga.com for more information about minimum order quantities and pricing tiers.
How should I care for my BlankSaga products? +
For best results, machine wash cold with like colors and tumble dry on low heat. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners. Our heavyweight materials are designed to maintain their shape and quality through many washes when cared for properly.
When will my order ship? +
Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-2 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped. During peak seasons, processing times may be slightly longer.
Can I cancel or modify my order? +
If you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact us immediately at support@blanksaga.com. We process orders quickly, so we can only make changes if your order hasn't been shipped yet.
